Gesture-Controlled Robot

Real-time hand-gesture controlled robot: a webcam feed is processed with OpenCV while MediaPipe detects 21 landmark points on the hand every frame. Raised-finger count classifies the gesture — one for forward, two for backward, three for left, four for right, fist to stop — and the command is sent wirelessly over Wi-Fi to an ESP32, which drives two motors through an L298N motor driver.
